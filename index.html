Company History

Early Bird Industries was founded in 2018 out a love for connecting with people through technology and design. The company was founded by Anna Lotko, who has a background in game design and web development. Her mission is to find the best projects with the best teams and dive in with creativity and code. Anna hopes to grow Early Bird Industries so that designers, developers, product managers and other tech-minded folks can collaborate and learn from each other.
